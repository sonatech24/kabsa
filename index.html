Kabsa

Ingredients


  • 2 cubes Maggi chicken stock
  • 1 kg whole chicken cutten into pieces
  • 800g rice ,washed and drained
  • 2 medium potatoes cutten into cubes
  • 2 medium onions chopped
  • ½ tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 medium tomatoes diced
  • ½ tbsp ground clove
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • ½ tbsp ground cardamom
  • 2 L water
  • ½ tbsp ground coriander
  • pinch of salt
  • 4 tbsp vegetable oil

Method


  • Step 1

    Put water and chicken in a large pot, bring to a boil. Cover and simmer over low heat for 25 minutes or until the chicken is almost cooked. Remove chicken from stock and set aside (reserve the stock).

  • Step 2

    In another large pot, heat vegetable oil and fry the cooked chicken pieces from all sides until they become golden color. Remove chicken and set it aside again.

  • Step 3

    In the same pot cook potato for 3 minutes then add onion and cook for another 5 minutes or until onions become golden color. Add spices, tomatoes, tomato paste and MAGGI chicken Stock cubes. Stir for 2 minutes.

  • Step 4

    Add the reserved stock to the pot, the stock should be around 7 cups or 1750ml. Add more water if stock is not enough. Add rice and the fried chicken; bring to a boil with occasional stirring. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes or until rice is cooked ,then serve immediately.
